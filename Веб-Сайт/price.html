Premium Access
to SpaceVision AI

SpaceVision AI is an advanced artificial intelligence system designed to support safe spacecraft navigation in space. The system uses onboard cameras to detect surrounding objects and debris in real time with unmatched precision.

By analyzing camera imagery, the AI determines the distance and relative position of objects and evaluates possible risks along the flight path.

Based on this analysis, SpaceVision AI recommends the safest direction — left, right, or straight ahead — helping avoid collisions at every stage of the mission.
