Intelligent Navigation System

How the AI Navigates

The AI collects real-time data from the onboard camera and converts raw visual input into structured spatial information about the environment.

By analyzing detected objects, distances, and motion vectors, the system builds a dynamic map of the surrounding space.

Based on this analysis, the AI determines how the spacecraft should adjust its trajectory to maintain safety and stability at all times.

The system then recommends precise maneuvers — shifting left, right, or continuing straight — executing collision avoidance instantly.
